October 18, 2014 - San Francisco, California

Since San Francisco offers such a fun array of activities and we have called this home for the past few years we are looking forward to celebrating our wedding weekend in a unique part of the city. With breathtaking views, rich historical presence, and a convenient location for exploring the Bay Area, the Presidio offers a romantic escape from the city. Check back frequently for additional details about the events.

Out of Town Guest Social

Out of town guests are invited to enjoy drinks and desserts on Friday evening at the beautiful Inn at the Presidio Formal invitation to be sent with details.

Ceremony

The ceremony will be held at the Chapel of Our Lady in the Presidio on Saturday afternoon.

Cocktail Hour and Reception

Immediately following the ceremony just down the path at the SF Film Center in the Presidio.

Send Off Picnic

Join us for a Picnic in the Presidio - one of our favorite weekend activities! We will save a spot with a pop-up tent, some blankets, and fold out chairs. Off the Grid picnic in the Presidio group provides an array of food trucks, local farmer booths, and a great bar set up for the public to enjoy on a casual Sunday afternoon. Stop by to enjoy the amazing views and say goodbye to the newlyweds.
