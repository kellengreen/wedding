Spend some time getting to know one of the most beautiful National Parks in the nation. There are many self guided tours and wonderful bike/walking paths to explore.

North Beach/ Pier 39

Where we call home, we have lived in the North Beach neighborhood since moving to SF in 2011. We live right at the base of beautiful Coit Tower and up the hill from Saint’s Peter and Paul church. If you are up for a delicious breakfast head to Mama’s- you will be sure to wait but said to had the best breakfast in town. While in the area make sure to check out Fisherman’s Wharf on pier 39 to take a look at the sea lions, beautiful views, aquatic park, and that’s about it! Don’t spend too much time here as it truly is a tourist trap!
