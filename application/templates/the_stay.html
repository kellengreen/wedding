House Rental

Airbnb is a trusted San Francisco community marketplace where people list their homes online for rental use. This would be a great option for a group traveling together or a couple looking for a studio for the weekend!

Flights

Feel free to fly into either Oakland International Airport OR San Francisco International - they are both convenient into the city! You can either take BART (est. $10-12 per person), shuttle service (est. $25 pp), cab (est. $50 ride), or Uber (est. $35 ride) into the city.

Getting Around

We would recommend not renting a car. Parking overnight in hotels or parking garages can get very expensive not to mention hard to find! The city is full of cabs and rideshare programs which we would encourage you to take advantage of.
