10 / 18 / 2014

Since San Francisco offers such a fun array of activities and we have called this home for the past few years we are looking forward to celebrating our wedding weekend in a unique part of the city. With breathtaking views, rich historical presence, and convenient location for exploring the Bay Area, the Presidio offers a romantic escape from the city. The wedding events will be held within the park, starting with an out of town guest social on the grounds of the Inn at the Presidio on Friday evening.

Saturday’s events will begin with an afternoon ceremony held at the Chapel of Our Lady with the cocktail hour and reception immediately following just down the path at the SF Film Center in the Presidio.

Hotels: We are still working on gathering room blocks at various hotels around the area and will post as soon as we have solidified. Please note the week of October 18th, there are a few very large events being hosted in San Francisco so hotel rooms will be limited, we recommend booking well in advance.

North Beach/ Pier 39

Where we call home, we have lived in the North Beach neighborhood since moving to SF in 2011. We live right at the base of beautiful Coit Tower and up the hill from Saint’s Peter and Paul church. If you are up for a delicious breakfast head to Mama’s- you will be sure to wait but said to had the best breakfast in town. While in the area make sure to check out Fisherman’s Wharf on pier 39 to take a look at the sea lions, beautiful views, aquatic park, and that’s about it! Don’t spend too much time here as it truly is a tourist trap!
