Kellen and Alison

Living in brother sister dorms freshman year at Gonzaga University and having many of the same mutual friends shockingly, our paths never really crossed until May of 2008! Our story really begins at 517 Mission Street- Alison's college house. As Senior year was coming to an end, the number of parties and BBQ's drastically increased as "real life" was creeping quickly around the corner. The ladies of 517 hosted one last BBQ before graduation where we met for the first time.

While neither of us were particularly interested in the other, at the time he actually started dating another friend of ours for the Summer. Kellen was around the house quite a bit and we started to get to know one another. As the rainiest summer in Spokane came to a close the so did his Summer fling with the other housemate at 517. As most of our friends didn't stay in Spokane to start their careers, the three amigos Stephanie Murphy, the two of us were excited to know that they had each other to hang out with.

As our time at 517 Mission quickly came to an end, a new adventure began as Stephanie and Alison got a "grown up" apartment up on the South Hill. Along with that "grown up" apartment came "grown up" furniture. As we were learning to be fiscally responsible (a total joke for those of you that know both Steph and Alison) the girls decided to use Craigslist to purchase a used sectional. As Kellen was the only man left in Spokane the girls were able to talk him into driving a U-Haul 45 minutes outside of the city to pick up what turned out to be the most disgusting pile of dog hair, feces, and dust! Kellen bit his tongue, loaded the couch and began the what should have been 45 min drive back to Spokane. Not only was he covered in lord knows what, but the freeway got closed while we were on our way back to town! We sat in an un-airconditioned U-Haul truck for over 2 hours with only AM radio, which made Kellen late for a date with another young lady! Little did he know that extended couch delivery would change his life forever, he got to know the woman he would spend the rest of his life with and create a point in time that changed his future forever. After the couch incident, Kellen didn't come around for a while.

As time went on and the girls bought new furniture, Kellen started to hang around quite a bit and introduced Nick Deccio (best man) to the ladies and the fabulous four was born. Whether it was nights out to Twigs and Waddells, dance nights out to Crazy 8's, or Karaoke nights at The Hangar, the four of us had one hell of a time together. As the two of us began to spend more time together we realized what great friends we had become and that there might be something more to our friendship. We went on our first "date" to see Slumdog Millionaire followed by a few Sam Adam's Winter Ale's at TGI Fridays out in the Valley. From that night forward our love saga began and 6 years later we are tying the knot!

Although our journey to where we are now wasn't always smooth. All of the ups and downs have proven time and time again that our friendship and love for one another is unbreakable. We have fallen madly in love with one another as we have supported tough family situations, promotions and new jobs, bad days and great ones, many moves, and most importantly each others hopes and dreams. Looking forward we are excited to start yet another new adventure and couldn't be more excited! We are each others rock and best friend, we can't wait to spend the rest of our lives together.
